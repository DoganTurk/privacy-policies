SubLedger — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 May 2026 · Effective immediately

SubLedger is built by Hüseyin Doğan Türk (the "Developer"). This document describes what data the app collects, why, and your rights. Read it before installing. If anything is unclear, email dogan.turk@sabanciuniv.edu.

1. Data we collect on your device only

All app-specific data — subscriptions, renewal history, FX-rate snapshots, settings, in-app purchase state — is stored exclusively on your device using Android's local storage (sqflite + SharedPreferences). This data never leaves your device. We have no servers; we cannot access it; it is removed when you uninstall the app.

2. Data collected by third parties

The app shows ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Device identifiers (Android Advertising ID)
  • Ad-interaction events (clicks, impressions, dismissals)

This collection is governed by Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy. We do not have access to this data; AdMob acts as an independent data controller. You can opt out of personalised advertising via Settings → Manage ad preferences within the app, or via Android's system Privacy → Ads menu.

3. Permissions the app requests

  • INTERNET + ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: required by AdMob to fetch ads and the UMP consent form. The app does not connect to any banking, payment, or third-party financial service.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Android 13+ permission for renewal-reminder notifications (3 days before a subscription renews).
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: re-arms renewal notifications after device reboot.

4. EU / UK users (GDPR)

On first launch, EU/UK users see a consent dialog (powered by Google's User Messaging Platform) asking whether AdMob may use device identifiers for personalised ads. You can decline; non-personalised ads still serve. You can withdraw or modify consent any time via Settings → Manage ad preferences. We act as a data controller only for any data we directly receive (none in SubLedger's case — see §1). AdMob acts as an independent controller for the data it collects.

5. California residents (CCPA)

You can request a copy of any personal information we hold about you, request its deletion, or opt out of its sale. Send these requests to dogan.turk@sabanciuniv.edu. Note: SubLedger does not "sell" personal information in the CCPA sense; we don't collect it on our servers in the first place.

6. Children

The app is rated for general audiences (13+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Account / no account

SubLedger does not require an account. You don't register; we don't have your name, email, or password. The only identifier we ever interact with is Google's Advertising ID, used by AdMob (above).

8. In-app purchases

The app offers a one-time non-consumable in-app purchase ("Remove Ads"). Payment is processed by Google Play Billing. We receive a confirmation that the purchase succeeded; we do not see your payment details. Refunds are handled by Google Play under their standard policy.

9. Data retention

Local app data persists until you uninstall the app or use the "Reset all game data" / equivalent option in Settings. AdMob data retention follows Google's policies; consult their privacy policy for details.

10. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy materially, we will update the "Last updated" date above and (for material changes) prompt you within the app on next launch. Continued use of the app after the change date constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints: dogan.turk@sabanciuniv.edu
